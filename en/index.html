About Us

NICORP d.o.o. is a construction company which provides services in all types of construction and construction-craft works within residential, commercial and industrial buildings, as well as the design and implementation of innovative architectural solutions.

Founded with the aim of making modern construction methods, enriched with modern architectural solutions, available to clients on the domestic market. From the very beginning, we've tried to justify the trust of our clients with a professional approach, respecting the budget, deadlines, as well as all contractual obligations.

Commitment to the highest standards and innovative solutions, with a team of experienced professionals, complemented by carefully chosen responsible subcontractors, led by highly qualified management staff, allows us to provide clients with the best solutions and implement all of their ideas.

Our success is defined by how quickly and efficiently we can exceed your expectations in providing top quality service and price.
